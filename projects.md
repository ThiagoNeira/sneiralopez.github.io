### Heston Stochastic Volatility Model

Implementation of the Heston stochastic volatility model for European option pricing and market calibration. The project compares multiple pricing methods and optimization strategies, and applies the model to real TSLA options data. This collaborative project was developed with Arindam Bhattacharyya , Ankit Kumar and Ethan Zhou.

**Highlights**

* Implemented European option pricing under the Heston model using Carr–Madan FFT and direct numerical integration.
* Developed a calibration pipeline combining differential evolution with nonlinear least-squares optimization.
* Benchmarked pricing accuracy, computational efficiency, and calibration robustness across numerical methods.
* Applied the calibration framework to real TSLA options market data after preprocessing and filtering option quotes.

**Technologies:** Python, NumPy, SciPy, Pandas

**Repository:** [GitHub](https://github.com/ThiagoNeira/Heston-Option-Pricing-Analysis)
